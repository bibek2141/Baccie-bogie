About Author

Sandra Glosser

AN INTERNATIONAL MOTIVATIONAL SPEAKER AND TRAINER, TELEVISION SHOW HOSTESS AND AUTHOR

My first experience as a writer started with the Dallas Morning News as an obituary writer. My bizarre sense of humor helped me maintain a sense of well being and the opportunity to write feature stories for the newspaper increased my appetite to become a writer. When I moved to Aspen, Colorado, I wrote a newspaper column and then penned a book about a local police officer who moved to the community as an undercover agent. I also enjoyed a fourteen-year experience as a host for my local television show. Writing became a hobby as I pursued my speaking career traveling across the country with my magnificent four-pound Maltese dog named Bacci Bogie.
